Privacy Policy

App Reddit Story Video Generator Effective July 15, 2026

This app is a personal project run by a single operator for their own YouTube and TikTok accounts. It has no user accounts, no analytics, and no data is sold or shared beyond what's needed to generate and publish a video.

1 Who this covers

This policy describes how the app handles data for its Operator — the one person who runs it, on their own computer, for their own connected accounts. There are no other users.

2 What data is processed

DataPurpose
Story text and titlesWritten by the Operator or generated by Claude, used as narration source material
Generated audio, images, videoThe output media produced for each video
OAuth access & refresh tokensYouTube and TikTok credentials, used only to publish videos the Operator has approved

3 Where data lives

Everything is stored locally on the Operator's own machine — generated media files, the review queue, and OAuth tokens. Nothing is sent to a remote database, no analytics or tracking scripts are included, and there is no server-side storage operated by anyone other than the Operator.

4 Third-party processors

To generate and publish a video, the app sends the relevant data to these services, each under its own privacy policy:

  • ElevenLabs — receives story text to synthesize narration audio
  • Anthropic (Claude) — receives story context to write stories, scene prompts, and post metadata
  • Pollinations.ai — receives a scene description to generate a background image
  • Google (YouTube Data API) — receives the finished video and metadata, only after Operator approval
  • TikTok (Content Posting API) — receives the finished video and metadata, only after Operator approval

5 OAuth tokens

YouTube and TikTok access tokens are stored in a local file that is never committed to source control and never transmitted anywhere except back to Google or TikTok's own APIs to authenticate a publish request the Operator approved.

6 Retention and deletion

The Operator can delete any locally stored data — generated videos, the review queue, or OAuth tokens — at any time by removing the corresponding files from their own machine. Revoking access from within your Google or TikTok account settings also invalidates the stored tokens.

7 Children's privacy

This app is not directed at, and is not knowingly used by, children under 13.

8 Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated as the project evolves. The effective date above reflects the most recent revision.
